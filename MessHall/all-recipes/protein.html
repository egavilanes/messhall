Protein peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Replenishing and tasty smoothie to gain energy or recover after a workout

~ Recipe by FitnessBlender ~

prep time

0 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Drinks

instructions

step 1

Pour milk into blades of the blender, followed by yogurt.

step 2

Add peeled banana then blend.

step 3

Add peanut butter and desired amount of ice, then finish blending.

ingredients

1/8 cup milk

3/4 cup plain yogurt

1 whole banana

3/4 cup ice

2 tbs peanut butter

tools

Blender
