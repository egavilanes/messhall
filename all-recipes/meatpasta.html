Penne Pasta with Meat Sauce

Comforting pasta recipe to enjoy with a filling sauce

~ Recipe by SimplyRecipes ~

prep time

10min.

cook time

45min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Dinner

instructions

step 1

Heat to boiling a large pot with 4 quarts of water in it. Once the water is boiling, salt it with 2 Tbsp of salt. Once the water returns to a boil add the pasta to the pot. Leave uncovered, let cook on high heat with a vigorous boil for 8-10 minutes, then drain.

step 2

Heat olive oil in a very large skillet on medium heat. Add the chopped onion, Italian seasoning and red pepper flakes. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the onions are softened. Add the garlic, fresh thyme, season with salt and pepper. Cook for an additional minute, until the garlic is fragrant. Remove from heat and set aside.

step 3

Heat a large pan on high heat. Salt the bottom of the pan generously. Once the pan is hot, break up small chunks of ground beef and add them to the pan, without stirring. (You may need to work in batches; do not crowd the pan.) Cook meat until well browned then flip meat and continue to cook until brown.

step 4

Add tomato sauce. Use the edge of your metal spatula to break up the bigger chunks of meat into smaller pieces. Add basil. Add a teaspoon of sugar. Bring to a simmer on low heat, let cook, uncovered, for 15 minutes.

step 5

Once the sauce has simmered for 15 minutes, adjust seasonings. Add salt and pepper to taste. Add a little more sugar if the sauce is too acidic. Add more tomato sauce if the sauce is a little dry. Stir in the cooked penne pasta. Sprinkle with chopped parsley. Serve immediately.

ingredients

1/2 cup of rolled oats

1/2 cup of chocolate milk (or unsweetened chocolate almond milk)

1 tablespoon cocoa powder

1 tablespoon chocolate chips

2 teaspoons chia seeds

1-2 teaspoons pure maple syrup or honey

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

dash of sea salt

tools
