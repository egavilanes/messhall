Almond Butter Banana Honey Toast

A quick and filling snack for energy

~ Recipe by TheChicLife ~

prep time

0 min.

cook time

3 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Snacks

instructions

step 1

Toast bread to preference

step 2

Top with nut butter, speading evenly to desired thickness.

step 3

Evenly spread sliced banana on top of the toast.

step 4

Drizzle honey on top, spinkle with cinnamon, and sprinkle with chia seeds, if using and enjoy!

ingredients

1 slice of whole grain bread

1-2 tablespoons nut butter

1 small sliced banana

1 teaspoon honey

1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon chia seeds, optional

tools

Butter knife

Measuring spoons
