Iced Instant Coffee

Very simple and affordable instant coffee recipe for something sweet and energizing in the morning! ~ Recipe by Maangchi ~

prep time

0 min.

cook time

3 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Drinks

instructions

step 1

Combine the coffee, powdered creamer, honey, and the water in a quick shaker or cup that can be covered.

step 2

Cover and shake for about 30 seconds until mixed well.

step 3

Pour into glass and add ice cubes. Stir well and serve.

ingredients

1 tablespoon instant coffee

2 tablespoons powdered creamer

1 tablespoon honey (or sugar)

1 cup cold water

1 cup ice cubes

tools

Quick shaker (optional)
