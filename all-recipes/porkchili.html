Pork Squash Chili

Hearty and flavorful meal to enjoy for lunch or dinner

~ Recipe by FitnessBlender ~

prep time

15 min.

cook time

45min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dinner

instructions

step 1

Trim the fat away away from pork and cut it into half-inch cubes

step 2

Slice the onion and caremelize it into oil for 5-6 minutes. Add the meat cut into cubes and garlic slices and brown them until they are cooked.

step 3

Add all other vegetables and spices and stir well

step 4

Pour in th etomatoes and cook for 30 minutes wiht the lid on

step 4

Chili is done when the cubes of squash are thoroughly cooked and tender

step 5

Serve with chip, bread, or tortillas or just enjoy it as soup

ingredients

8 ounces butternut squash

12 ounces boneless pork chops

15 ounces black and kidney beans

30 ounces stewed tomatoes

1 cup corn

1 large onion

1 cup mushrooms

1 cup water

1 tbsp chili powder

1 tsp cumin

1/4 tsp oregano

2 tbsp olive oil

3 cloves garlic

1/2 tsp cinnamon

tools

large pot

large wooden spoon/Spatula
