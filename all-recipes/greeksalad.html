Greek Chicken Salad

Refreshing salad to last for several meals

~ Recipe by HealthyFitnessMeals ~

prep time

10min.

cook time

20min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Lunch

instructions

step 1

Place chicken breast in a ziplock bag.

step 2

Add all the dressing/marinade ingredients to a small jar. Place the lid on and shake well until emulsified. Pour 1/3 of this mixture over the chicken and mix to combine. Keep refrigerated to marinate for at least 1 hour.

step 3

Keep remaining 2/3 of the mixture in the jar, refrigerated, to use as a dressing.

step 4

Heat a large non-stick pan or grill pan over medium heat. Add chicken and cook for 8-10 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.

step 5

Transfer the chicken on a place and allow it to sit covered for about 10-12 minutes, then slice it.

step 6

Add the lettuce to a large serving bowl.

step 7

Top with tomoatoes, cucumbers, onion, olives, and sliced chicken. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese.

step 8

Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve immediately and enjoy!

ingredients

6 cups Romaine lettuce (chopped)

1 pound chicken breast

2 cups cherry tomatoes

1 cup Persian or seedless cucumber

1/2 medium red onion

1/2 cup Kalamata olives

1/2 cup feta cheese

few spears of fresh oregano leaves (optional)

For the Greek Dressing

1/24 cup extra virgin olive oil

3 tbsp red wine vinegar

1 garlic clove

2 teaspoons dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

1/4 easpoon freshly ground black pepper

tools

ziplock bag

large serving bowl

jar
