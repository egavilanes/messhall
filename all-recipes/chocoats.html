Triple Chocolate Overnight Oats

Sweet and rich oatmeal recipe for fast mornings

~ Recipe by Lemons+Zest ~

prep time

5 min.

cook time

4hr 5min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Breakfast

instructions

step 1

In a small jar or bowl mix together all ingrdients well to combine.

step 2

Seal closed and chill overnight or at least 4 hours.

step 3

If too dry when ready to eat, add another tablespoon or so of milk.

step 4

Eat warmed up or straight from the fridge!

ingredients

1/2 cup of rolled oats

1/2 cup of chocolate milk (or unsweetened chocolate almond milk)

1 tablespoon cocoa powder

1 tablespoon chocolate chips

2 teaspoons chia seeds

1-2 teaspoons pure maple syrup or honey

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

dash of sea salt

tools

jar or container for oatmeal
