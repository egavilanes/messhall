Mixtas (Guatemalan Hot Dogs)

Guatemalan street food recipe for a quick and tasty lunch

~ NELady ~

prep time

5 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Tags : Lunch

instructions

step 1

Heat hot dogs

step 2

Cook chopped cabbage in salted water until tender

step 3

Heat flour tortillas then top with hot dogs, cabbage, then guacamole

ingredients

10 hot dogs

10 flour tortillas

1 small cabbage, thinly sliced

guacamole

3 ripe avocados

1-2 tbsn lemon juice

3/4 teaspoon garlic salt

tools

frying pan
