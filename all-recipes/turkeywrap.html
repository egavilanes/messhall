California Turkey Club Wrap

Healthy and satisfying lunch for on-the-go

~ Recipe by TastesLovely ~

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

1 serving

Tags : Lunch

instructions

step 1

Assemble the turkey wrap by layering in the order the ingredients are listed. Wrap, cut, and serve.

step 2

Fold two sides of wrap towards the center then roll one unfolded side to the oppisite to wrap the turkey wrap.

step 3

Cut wrap in the middle and enjoy.

ingredients

1 spinach tortilla wrap

1 tbsp mayonnaise

1 tspn mustard

2 ounces turkey lunch meat

2 slices of cooked bacon

2 ounces avocado, sliced

2 slices of tomato

1 cup of shredded iceberg lettuce

tools

Butter knife
