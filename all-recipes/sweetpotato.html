Baked Japanese Sweet Potatoes

Simple baking recipe to enjoy a nice snack

~ Recipe by Just One Cookbook ~

prep time

5min.

cook time

1hr 30min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Snacks

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 325 F

step 2

Gently but thoroughly wash potatoes well with a brush

step 3

Poke a few holes in the potatoes and then wrap in foil

step 4

Bake for 90 minutes then remove foil and enjoy with or without potato skin

ingredients

4 Japanese sweet potatoes

tools

baking sheet

parchment paper
